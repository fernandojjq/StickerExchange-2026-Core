# Swap-26: Complete Guide for Content Creators

## Project Overview

Swap-26 is a Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to manage and facilitate the exchange of physical stickers from the official FIFA World Cup 2026 album.

The app allows users to:

* Track their sticker inventory (duplicates and missing stickers)
* Generate QR codes representing their collection to share with others
* Scan other users’ QR codes to find matching trades
* Perform exchanges offline (via QR codes) or live (via Firebase real-time synchronization)

## Application Sections

### Section 1: Album (Inventory)

Main area where users manage their collection.

**Features:**

* Search by sticker number or country
* Filters:

  * ALL
  * MISSING
  * DUPLICATES
* Type filters:

  * ALL
  * BADGES
  * PROMO (C1–C14)
  * EXTRAS (E1–E20)
  * PLAYERS

**Inventory Stats:**

* Total unique stickers
* Number of duplicates
* Completion progress

**Sticker Management:**

* Tap + to add duplicates
* Tap – to remove duplicates
* Long press to mark as priority

---

### Section 2: Swap Center

Two exchange modes:

#### Offline Mode (QR Codes)

* Generate QR code from your duplicates
* Others scan it to find matches
* No internet required

#### Live Mode (Firebase Realtime Database)

* Create a room with a unique code
* Join via code or QR
* Real-time synchronization
* Both users must confirm exchanges

---

### Exchange Flow

1. Scan friend’s QR → View matches
2. Select stickers you want
3. Select stickers you offer
4. Friend does the same
5. Both confirm
6. Inventories update automatically

---

### Section 3: My Profile (My ID)

* Custom username
* Personal QR code
* Stats:

  * Total stickers
  * Unique stickers
  * Total trades
* Trade history
